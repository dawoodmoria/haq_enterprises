Our Story

Who we are

Haq Enterprises was founded with the objective of introducing and distributing advanced healthcare and allied products through a disciplined, documentation-led market approach. Over twenty years, we have evolved into a full-scope institutional supplier trusted by government hospitals, private clinics, laboratories, and NGOs across Karachi and Sindh.

Our scope spans medicines, medical equipment, surgical instruments, hospital furniture, and general supply categories — all executed through verified procurement channels and supported by structured logistics and after-sales operations.

Our Journey

Built over two decades

What began as a focused distribution effort has grown into a multi-category institutional supply operation — shaped by the needs of Pakistan's healthcare procurement landscape and the trust placed in us by public sector institutions across Sindh.

Each milestone reflects an operational commitment: adding capability not to expand rapidly, but to serve institutional clients with greater depth and reliability at every stage.
