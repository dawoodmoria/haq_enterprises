How we work

Our procurement workflow

A four-step structured process ensures every supply engagement is documented, verified, and executed reliably.

1
Requirement Analysis

Institutional procurement needs are documented — item specifications, quantities, delivery location, and timeline are captured and reviewed.

2
Sourcing & Verification

Items are sourced through verified, licensed supplier channels — with quality checks and batch documentation prepared for institutional review.

3
Documentation & Compliance

All supply documentation is prepared — invoices, delivery notes, certificates, and compliance records — aligned to procurement office requirements.

4
Delivery & After-Sales

Structured delivery to the institutional location with delivery confirmation, follow-up support, and recurring supply coordination as required.
